iPhone Chess is a JavaScript chess adaptation written by Per Digre.

  • Development at https://sourceforge.net/projects/iphonegamez
  • BSD, Apache and Public Domain license
  • Built on John Hewitt's iUI available from http://code.google.com/p/iui/
  • opensource chess images

Notes

  • Supports Castling and En Passant.
  • Castling is done by moving the king, rook moves automatically.
  • When swapping sides computer makes its move instantly (since it's its turn) so you may be disoriented at first and not notice it. Also undo moves you will have to do it twice since you first get the computers side.
