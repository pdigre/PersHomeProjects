iPhone BlackJack is a JavaScript BlackJack Trainer written by Per Digre.

  • *** COMING SOON ***
  • Development at https://sourceforge.net/projects/iphonegamez
  • BSD, Apache and Public Domain license
  • Built on John Hewitt's iUI available from http://code.google.com/p/iui/
  • royalty free card images

Notes

  • Teach modes: basic strategy, simple card-count, advanced cardcount.
  • Betting strategies follows Mensa's advice for such as Stand on 16, Split 10s, Insurance, etc
  • 6 deck standard with 1 deck cut
  • Computer player can be set to Basic or Mensa Simple / Advanced
